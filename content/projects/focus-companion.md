## Public framing

Many people do not need another task manager. They need a gentler bridge into action.

This project explores a companion-style interface for starting, returning, and staying with work without shame-heavy productivity language.

## What it is for

- Helping a person begin a task.
- Supporting short focus sessions.
- Making return-after-distraction easier.
- Creating soft accountability without pretending to be a medical tool.

## What it is not

- Not a treatment or diagnosis tool.
- Not a replacement for care, coaching, or clinical support.
- Not another dashboard that assumes planning is the whole problem.

## Questions

- What tone helps someone start without feeling managed?
- What should the companion remember across sessions?
- When should the interface be ambient instead of demanding attention?
