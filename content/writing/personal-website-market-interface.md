## Draft note

A traditional portfolio usually answers one question: what have I done?

That matters, but it is not the whole thing I want this site to do.

I want the site to answer a more active question: what kinds of problems am I becoming good at noticing?

Right now, the pattern across my projects is AI systems that help humans act in messy real-world contexts. Browser workflows break. Stories branch. Characters drift. Attention does not always obey a task list. Good opportunities do not always announce themselves cleanly.

So the site is organized less like a trophy shelf and more like a map of current direction.

It should make the work easier to inspect, easier to connect, and easier to trust.
